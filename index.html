Borders and Border-radius

Here are some important things to know about borders:

  • Borders are supported on most main browsers
  • There are three primary properties to borders, border-width, border-style, border-color

    • Border Properties

    Style

    The style is where you can really have fun with borders, here are a few options.

    • Dotted
    • Dashed
    • Solid
    • Double
    • Groove
    • Inset
    • Ridge
    • Outset
